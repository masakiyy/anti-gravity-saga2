```
◆作品概要
本作は、空が崩落した近未来の廃墟を舞台に、孤独な少年ルカと、記憶を失ったエルフ
シルアが出会い、失われた「重力」を取り戻す旅に出る、全3幕11章のファンタジー
アニメーションです。パイロット版では第1〜2章のエッセンスを5分に凝縮し、クライ
マックスで流れる「名前のない歌」と、そこから浮かび上がる重力の喪失を描きます。

◆本作の特徴
・個人クリエイター × AI補助 × 外部プロフェッショナル（声優・作曲家）の協働制作
・全46枚のキービジュアルを Img2Video / モーションコミック手法で動画化
・架空言語による歌唱シーン（原作オリジナルの作詞）
・全11章の世界観を詳細に設計した原案（キャラクターデザイン5種・背景設定8種）

◆公開情報
```

## Press Release (English Draft)

```
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
October 17, 2026

Individual Creator Launches 5-Minute Animated Pilot "Anti-Gravity Saga" on YouTube
Crowdfunding Campaign Begins Same Day

──────────────────────────────────────

A 5-Minute Window into an 11-Chapter Epic
"From the day the sky fell..."

Individual creator [Creator Name] has released the pilot episode (5 minutes) of
their original animation project, "Anti-Gravity Saga," on YouTube today,
October 17, 2026. A crowdfunding campaign on CAMPFIRE to fund full production
also launches today.

◆About the Work
Set in a post-catastrophe world where the sky itself has collapsed,
"Anti-Gravity Saga" tells the story of a lonely boy Luca and a memoryless elf
Silua, whose encounter sparks a journey to recover what gravity once meant.
The full project spans three acts and eleven chapters. The pilot distills
the first two chapters, culminating in "The Nameless Song"—sung in a fictional
language—and the quiet, weightless moment that follows.

◆Production Approach
- Solo creator direction, with AI-assisted animation and human professional
  voice acting and original music composition
- 46 original key visuals animated via Img2Video and motion comic techniques
- Original lyrics in a constructed language for the core vocal scene
- Full 11-chapter worldbuilding with 5 character designs and 8 environment sets

◆Release Details
- Title: Anti-Gravity Saga
- Format: 5-minute animated pilot
- Distribution: YouTube (free, Japanese/English subtitles)
- Video URL: [YouTube URL]
- Official Site: https://anti-gravity-saga2.vercel.app

◆Crowdfunding
- Platform: CAMPFIRE (Japan) / cross-promoted on Kickstarter for international
- Duration: October 17 – November 30, 2026
- First Goal: JPY 2,000,000 (Episode 1 production funding, approx. USD 14,000)
- Stretch Goals: JPY 5M (Act 1 completion) / JPY 10M (premium voice cast)
- Rewards: 8 tiers from JPY 1,000 to JPY 500,000

◆Creator Statement
"The phrase 'the sky has fallen' evokes despair, but I wanted to depict it as
the moment when something new becomes visible. The pilot is a 5-minute door
into that world. The full series will trace Luca and Silua's journey across
all 11 chapters to recover the memory of gravity itself."
```
